WHAT WE DO

Chelsea Capital provides Government related projects which includes Oil & Gas trading, Infrastructure in Oil and Gas Management, Consultancy and Funding, particularly for large infrastructure and commercial Government projects. Chelsea Capital Limited has its head office in London (UK), with our Board Members, Staff and Agents being located across the Europe, China, Asia and Middle East.

We have earned a strong reputation amongst our clients, with privacy, reliability , efficiency and integrity being four pillars of success, built on a foundation of hard work and a desire to succeed.
