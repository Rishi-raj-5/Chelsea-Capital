WHAT WE DO

Mr Kunal Pushkarna over a period of last 30 years, with his management team have built a wealth of experience in purchasing, designing, developing and selling a range of commercial and residential properties in London. In September 2016, Mr.Kunal Pushkarna & Mr Tao Feng came together and formed Chelsea Capital.

Chelsea Capital Limited is a private limited company that provides project related services including Management, Consultancy and Funding, particularly for large infrastructure and commercial Government projects. Chelsea Capital Limited has its head office in London (UK), with our Board Members, Staff and Agents being located across the Europe, China, Asia and Middle East.

We now serve a range of high- end clientele, from wealthy individuals, through to prominent families and Investment Funds/Trusts. These clients are either purchasing or disposing of prime Real Estate, predominantly in Central London, but also in their home countries. We work closely with our clients to determine their requirements, and create bespoke services to meet their needs.

We have earned a strong reputation amongst our clients, with privacy, reliability , efficiency and integrity being four pillars of success, built on a foundation of hard work and a desire to succeed.

Types of Project

Our services are suitable for all types of Infrastructure Projects in either Greenfield and Brownfield including Sea Ports, Airports, Railway, Bridges, Roads and Highways, Water and Sanitation Utilities, Energy, Communications, Hospitals, Schools and Social Infrastructure etc. This includes other projects or activities that impact positively on the relevant country's basic infrastructure including the manufacturing of components used in infrastructure like cement and steel and infrastructure associated with Mining and agribusiness.

Projects can be either BOOT (Build, Own, Operate, Transfer) BOO (Build Own Operate), BOT (Build Own Transfer), BTO (Build, Transfer, Own), BT (Build Transfer), BLT (Build, Lease, Transfer), BRT (Build, Rent, Transfer) or PPP (Public Private Partnership) and EPC with Finance model as well.

We can offer expertise in development of Smart Cities with the state of the art infrastructure connected with the latest technologies from around the world.We can provide renewable energy solution in Solar and home lighting systems along with wireless power transmission.

Any infrastructure projects needing EPC and funding can be provided upto $ 10 billion. We have excess to big companies from China and the middle east for the same.
