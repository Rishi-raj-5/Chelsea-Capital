SOME OF OUR RECENT WORK


Interiors for the Studio Apartments below were designed to maximise the available space, almost creating an "illusion" making the space feel larger than its physical size.

The Earls Court Studio includes a feature wall, and the bedroom was built on a mezzanine floor. The Ground floor area is 340 sqft, with the mezzanine adding another 66 sqft.

The Buckingham Gate Studio measured a mere 165 sqft in total, in which a lounge, bedroom, kitchen and bathroom was created. This was achieved by concealing the bed in the lounge ceiling, which descends via remote control. The mechanism was specially made in Germany, whilst the furniture was hand- made in Italy at the precise height to accommodate the bed.
