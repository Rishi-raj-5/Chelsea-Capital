Installations:


  • Unscrew the M6 hand screw and remove the end cover.
  • Unscrew the M5 hand screws and open the revolvable cover.
  • Loosen the M4 screws on the cable clip and draw out the cable.
  • Loosen the M10x35 socket cap Screws on the tube straps.
  • Draw the pole under the tube straps.Then tighten up the screws on them.
  • Fix the AC input cable under the cable clip and tighten up the screw.
  • Connect the ground, neutral and live wires to the AC input (make sure it sufficiently grounded).
  • Close the main cover and tighten up the screws on it.
  • Fix the end cover back and tighten up the screws on it.





Maintenance:


  • Unscrew the M5 hand screw and Open the main cover.
  • Disconnect the driver from the wires of AC input.
  • Unscrew the waterproof connector for the driver.
  • Unscrew the M5x20 screws snd remove the fixing accessories on the driver.
  • Replace the failed driver with a new one. Tighten up all the screws and the Connector. Re-connect to the wires of AC input.
  • Close the main cover and tighten up the screws on it.



Module:


  • Unscrew the M5 hand screw and open the main cover.
  • Unscrew the M4x10 screws at the ends of the failed module.
  • Unscrew the male connector from the female connector.
  • Replace the module with a new one.
  • Align and tighten up the connector, and tighten up the screws.
  • Close the main cover and tightenup the screws on it.



Connectors Operation Guide:


Spin the male terminal clockwise. When the male indicatrix points between indicatrix female #2 and #3, and the gap between male and female terminals is extremely small, the connectors are well connected; otherwise, there will be risks in its waterproof performance.
When the gap between male and female terminals is extremely small, if any looseness can be sensed, please spin the male terminal clockwise until tight.
