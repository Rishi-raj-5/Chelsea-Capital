SOLAR STREET LIGHT


SOLAR ENERGY


Despite the abundant sun shine, solar energy is a vastly underutilised resource. However recent developments suggest that policy makers are beginning recognise the valuable role of solar energy in meeting the ever growing electricity demand.

Chelsea Capital Limited has recognised this momentum and is offering environment friendly, energy and money saving products powered by solar energy. Chelsea Capital limited has created the product design and are manufacturing products of high quality powered by solar energy and are offering them at competitive prices.

Solar kit : A must for every household. It consists of a tube light, an emergency lamp and a mini fan and UBS Port to charge your Mobile , Laptop etc all powered by a solar panel.

Street Light : Essential for all cities, towns and villages. This is a tool less maintenance product with flexible combination of modules and high efficiency LED light source powered by a solar panel.



Main Features


  • Modular pluggable technology, tool-free onsite maintenance
  • Double-coupling IP68 protection, higher waterproof level
  • Saving energy, protecting environment, being easily installed
  • Multi-circuit constant-current driver, single module hot- pluggable
  • Free serialisation and various power solutions

Light Distribution Curve


The light and colour scheme of its products comply with road operators’ visual habits. The products adopt equal brightness optical design in full consideration that it is unavailable to detect illumination by eye because human’s eye is sensitive to brightness, thus not only eliminating the visual bright spots and dark spots on road surface, and also bringing road operators comfortable operation, as well as improving lights illumination and obtaining accurate detected values.



Manual Instruction


  • Choosing Site:
    Choosing the best direction of lamp-house in order to receive the most solar energy. The solar street lamp must be installed in the site with best sunshine and without tree shade or building shade. It must have wonderful sunshine during 9:30AM to 3:30PM It should keep distance from hot source, or its life will be shortened. The solar panel should not be irradiated by other lighting in the evening ,or it will not work because of receiving the wrong order. There should have no other establishment(cable or conduit) underground the solar street lamp.

Remark


  • The installation and maintenance must be carried out by corresponding qualified technical staff.
  • Please do not use other components to do maintenance but without the proposal of its original manufacture. Or the system might be damaged.
  • All components of solar street lamp should not be damaged in the installation process.
  • Battery should not be inverted or placed in the humid or under sunlight directly in the transport and installation process.
  • Not short-circuit the wiring connections (the cathode touch the anode)
  • Please notice the wire should not clip in the conjunction of the pole while pulling the wire. The wire conjunction must link firm and tie up with PVC.
